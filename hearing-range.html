Hearing Range

Find the lowest and highest frequencies you can actually hear. Pair with the Mic Range demo on a second phone to compare what the speakers emit vs. what mics receive.

Two tests in one. With headphones, this measures your hearing. Without, you're measuring whatever the phone speaker can produce — most attenuate hard below ~150 Hz and above ~17 kHz. Start with low volume and raise it.

Slider is logarithmic over 20 Hz – 22 kHz. Tone is generated with Web Audio OscillatorNode, ramped on/off to avoid clicks. Volume cap is 50% of full output regardless of slider, so cranking the slider to 100% won't blow speakers — but use sane volume anyway, especially below 60 Hz where energy can be felt before heard.
